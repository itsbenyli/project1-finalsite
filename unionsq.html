Union Square

This stone comes from Union Square. It’s the chaotic heart of downtown Manhattan where every subway line seems to collide.

On Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, and Saturdays, the Greenmarket takes over. You can find organic honey, local apples, and some of the best bread in the city right next to professional chess players and street performers.

It’s a place of protest, celebration, and constant motion. Grab a coffee, sit on the steps, and watch the entire world walk past you in fifteen minutes.
