Hudson Yards

This stone is from Hudson Yards. I recommend this spot if you are looking for that futuristic and high end New York vibe. It feels like stepping into a sci fi movie with all the glass and steel everywhere.


You should definitely walk the High Line all the way to the end to get here. It is such a cool way to transition into this part of the city.


Even if you are just window shopping the scale of the Vessel and the views of the Hudson River at sunset are absolutely worth the trip.
